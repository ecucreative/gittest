The Arts at ECU

From stadium concerts to intimate gallery exhibitions, there are several hundred arts events to which students, employees and the public are invited, annually. Explore our gallery spaces and our public art. Attend iron pour. Catch a movie-either made in Hollywood or crafted on campus. Enjoy dance, musical theatre, classical plays and live performance in venues large and small. Hear our award-winning student ensembles as well as international-class professional touring atttractions. Make your own glass art in out custom facility. Enroll children in summer drama, band and art camps. Explore our programs and connect to the arts that inspire you most.
