Our Leadership

From studying in the hallowed halls of Oxford University to the storied classrooms at Harvard University, ECU’s administrators and faculty bring a wealth of knowledge, best practices, and innovative perspectives to our campuses, and to you, our students. ECU’s leadership includes experts in their fields and world-reknowned names in everything from literature to theology, from computer sciences to cardiology-so you are learning from the best. You’ll find academic programs, botrh undergraduate and graduate, that have attracted a wide variety of talented professionals, academicians, scientists, researchers, artists, and educators-offering global views and unique experiences that prepare tomorrow’s leaders for an international economy.
